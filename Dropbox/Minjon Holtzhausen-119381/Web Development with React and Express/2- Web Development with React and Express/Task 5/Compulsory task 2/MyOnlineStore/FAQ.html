Help Centre

Delivering a local package

Local packages take 1 to 3 days to be delivered. See our Shipping Policy for more detailed information


Payment options

We do not provide a Cash on Delivery option. The payment option include doing an EFT, paying with a credit or debit card, or via PayPal.

Return policy

We only accept returns of non-custom products within 15 days of delivery, a percentage refund will be given depending on the state of the product after we have received it. Please note, that no refunds are available for custom order products.
